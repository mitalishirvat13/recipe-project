Shahi Paneer

A royal Mughlai dish with paneer cubes cooked in a rich cashew-cream gravy.

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer (cubes)
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 10 cashews (soaked)
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp red chili, 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Heat ghee, sauté onions & cashews until golden.
  2. Add tomatoes, cook well & blend into a smooth paste.
  3. Cook paste with ginger-garlic and spices.
  4. Add paneer cubes & cream, simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Garnish with coriander & serve hot with naan/paratha.

Paneer Lababdar

A rich, flavorful North Indian curry made with paneer, tomatoes, cream & spices.

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer (cubes + some grated)
  • 2 tomatoes (puree)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 10 cashews (soaked & blended)
  • 1 tsp coriander powder, 1 tsp red chili, 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp butter/oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Heat butter, sauté onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomato puree & cook well.
  3. Mix in cashew paste & dry spices, cook until thick.
  4. Add paneer cubes + grated paneer & simmer 5 mins.
  5. Stir in cream, garnish with coriander & serve hot.

Malai Kofta

Crispy cottage cheese & potato balls in a creamy tomato-cashew gravy.

Ingredients:

  • 2 potatoes (boiled & mashed)
  • 100g paneer (grated)
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 10 cashews (soaked)
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric, chili powder, garam masala
  • Salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Mix mashed potato, paneer & cornflour. Shape into balls.
  2. Deep fry until golden brown. Keep aside.
  3. Sauté onions, cashews & tomatoes. Blend into paste.
  4. Cook paste with spices, ginger-garlic & cream.
  5. Just before serving, add koftas into hot gravy.
  6. Garnish with cream & coriander, serve with naan/rice.
