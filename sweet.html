Kaju Katli

Classic Indian cashew fudge with a rich melt-in-mouth texture.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cashews
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tsp ghee
  • Silver vark (optional)

Steps:

  1. Grind cashews into a fine powder.
  2. Boil sugar & water till 1-string consistency.
  3. Add cashew powder, cook till soft dough forms.
  4. Grease surface with ghee, roll & cut into diamonds.
  5. Decorate with silver vark & serve.

Gulab Jamun

Soft, spongy milk-solid balls soaked in fragrant sugar syrup.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup khoya (mawa)
  • 2 tbsp maida
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • Oil/ghee for frying
  • 1 cup sugar, 1 cup water
  • 2-3 cardamoms, few saffron strands

Steps:

  1. Mix khoya, maida & soda into soft dough.
  2. Shape into smooth balls without cracks.
  3. Deep fry on low flame until golden.
  4. Prepare sugar syrup with cardamom & saffron.
  5. Soak fried balls in warm syrup for 2 hrs.
  6. Serve warm or chilled.

Rasmalai

Soft chenna patties soaked in saffron flavored milk (rabri) and garnished with pistachios.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 liters milk
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice / vinegar
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 cups water
  • 4 cardamoms
  • A few saffron strands
  • Chopped pistachios & almonds

Steps:

  1. Boil milk & curdle with lemon juice. Drain & hang chenna.
  2. Knead chenna till smooth, shape into small flat discs.
  3. Boil sugar, water & cardamom in a deep pan.
  4. Add discs & cook covered for 12–15 mins.
  5. Prepare saffron milk (rabri) with sugar & nuts.
  6. Squeeze cooked discs & soak in rabri.
  7. Chill for 2–3 hours before serving.
