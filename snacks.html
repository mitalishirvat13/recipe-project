Paneer Butter Masala

A creamy, rich North Indian curry made with paneer and tomato gravy.

Ingredients:

  • Paneer - 200g
  • Butter - 2 tbsp
  • Tomato Puree - 1 cup

Steps:

  1. Heat butter and add spices.
  2. Cook tomato puree.
  3. Add paneer cubes & simmer.

White Sauce Pasta

Delicious pasta cooked in creamy white sauce with veggies.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta - 1 cup
  • Milk - 1 cup
  • Cheese - 1/2 cup

Steps:

  1. Boil pasta.
  2. Prepare white sauce.
  3. Mix pasta with sauce & serve.
