Steamed Momos

Soft and juicy dumplings filled with vegetables or meat, popular in Himalayan cuisine.

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour - 2 cups
  • Water - 3/4 cup (for dough)
  • Vegetables (cabbage, carrot, capsicum) - 1 cup finely chopped
  • Onion - 1 small, finely chopped
  • Garlic - 2 cloves, minced
  • Soy sauce - 1 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - 1 tsp (optional)

Steps:

  1. Mix flour with water and knead into a soft dough. Rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare the filling: sauté vegetables with garlic, soy sauce, salt, and a little oil.
  3. Roll dough into small circles, place filling in the center, and fold into dumplings.
  4. Steam the momos for 10–12 minutes until cooked.
  5. Serve hot with chili sauce or tomato chutney.

White Sauce Pasta

Delicious pasta cooked in creamy white sauce with veggies.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta - 1 cup
  • Milk - 1 cup
  • Cheese - 1/2 cup

Steps:

  1. Boil pasta.
  2. Prepare white sauce.
  3. Mix pasta with sauce & serve.

Chocolate Cake

Soft, moist, and rich chocolate cake perfect for celebrations.

Ingredients:

  • Flour - 1.5 cups
  • Cocoa Powder - 1/2 cup
  • Sugar - 1 cup
  • Butter - 1/2 cup
  • Milk - 1 cup

Steps:

  1. Mix dry ingredients.
  2. Add wet ingredients & blend well.
  3. Bake at 180°C for 30 mins.
