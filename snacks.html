Steamed Momos

Soft and juicy dumplings filled with vegetables or meat, popular in Himalayan cuisine.

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour - 2 cups
  • Water - 3/4 cup (for dough)
  • Vegetables (cabbage, carrot, capsicum) - 1 cup finely chopped
  • Onion - 1 small, finely chopped
  • Garlic - 2 cloves, minced
  • Soy sauce - 1 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - 1 tsp (optional)

Steps:

  1. Mix flour with water and knead into a soft dough. Rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare the filling: sauté vegetables with garlic, soy sauce, salt, and a little oil.
  3. Roll dough into small circles, place filling in the center, and fold into dumplings.
  4. Steam the momos for 10–12 minutes until cooked.
  5. Serve hot with chili sauce or tomato chutney.

Burger

A delicious burger with a juicy patty, fresh veggies, and melted cheese.

Ingredients:

  • Minced meat or veggie patty - 250g
  • Burger buns - 2
  • Lettuce leaves - 2
  • Tomato slices - 2
  • Onion slices - 2
  • Cheese slices - 2
  • Ketchup or mayonnaise - as needed
  • Salt & pepper - to taste

Steps:

  1. Mix meat/veggie with salt, pepper, and optional egg. Shape into patties.
  2. Cook patties on a pan for 4-5 minutes each side until done.
  3. Toast the burger buns lightly.
  4. Assemble: bottom bun → lettuce → patty → cheese → tomato → onion → top bun.
  5. Serve hot with ketchup or mayo.

Sandwich

Quick and tasty sandwich with fresh veggies and your choice of filling.

Ingredients:

  • Bread slices - 2
  • Butter - 1 tsp
  • Lettuce leaves - 1-2
  • Tomato slices - 2
  • Cucumber slices - 4-5
  • Cheese slice - 1
  • Salt & pepper - to taste
  • Optional: ketchup or mayonnaise

Steps:

  1. Spread butter (and optional ketchup/mayo) on the bread slices.
  2. Layer lettuce, tomato, cucumber, and cheese on one slice.
  3. Sprinkle salt and pepper.
  4. Cover with the second bread slice.
  5. Serve fresh or toast if desired.
