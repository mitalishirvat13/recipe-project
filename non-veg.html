Chicken Biryani

Fragrant rice layered with marinated chicken, aromatic spices, and saffron for a royal treat.

Ingredients:

  • Basmati rice – 2 cups
  • Chicken – 500 g
  • Yogurt – 1/2 cup
  • Onions – 2 large (sliced & fried)
  • Ginger-garlic paste – 2 tbsp
  • Biryani masala – 2 tbsp
  • Milk with saffron – 1/4 cup
  • Ghee – 2 tbsp
  • Fresh coriander & mint – handful
  • Salt – to taste

Steps:

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices for 1 hour.
  2. Cook rice till 70% done. Drain and keep aside.
  3. Cook chicken till tender with masala and fried onions.
  4. Layer rice and chicken in a pot, sprinkle saffron milk, coriander, mint, and ghee.
  5. Dum cook for 20 minutes on low heat. Serve hot with raita.

Butter Chicken

Juicy chicken simmered in a creamy tomato-based sauce with butter and spices.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken (boneless) – 500 g
  • Tomatoes – 4 (pureed)
  • Cream – 1/2 cup
  • Butter – 3 tbsp
  • Onion – 1 (chopped)
  • Ginger-garlic paste – 1 tbsp
  • Kasuri methi – 1 tsp
  • Red chili powder – 1 tsp
  • Garam masala – 1 tsp
  • Salt – to taste

Steps:

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, chili, and spices. Grill or pan-fry till cooked.
  2. Sauté onion in butter, add ginger-garlic, then tomato puree. Cook till thick.
  3. Add cream, butter, kasuri methi, and garam masala.
  4. Add chicken pieces, simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Serve with naan or rice.

Fish Fry

Crispy and spicy pan-fried fish fillets, perfect as a starter or side dish.

Ingredients:

  • Fish fillets – 500 g
  • Turmeric – 1/2 tsp
  • Red chili powder – 1 tsp
  • Lemon juice – 1 tbsp
  • Ginger-garlic paste – 1 tbsp
  • Rice flour – 2 tbsp (for crispiness)
  • Salt – to taste
  • Oil – for shallow frying

Steps:

  1. Marinate fish with turmeric, chili powder, lemon juice, salt, and ginger-garlic paste for 30 minutes.
  2. Coat lightly with rice flour.
  3. Shallow fry in hot oil till golden and crispy on both sides.
  4. Serve with onion rings and lemon wedges.
