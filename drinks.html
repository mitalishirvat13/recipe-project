Mango Mastani

A rich and creamy mango milkshake topped with ice cream, nuts, and cherries.

Ingredients:

  • Ripe mango - 2 (peeled & chopped)
  • Milk - 1 cup (chilled)
  • Sugar - 2 tbsp
  • Vanilla ice cream - 2 scoops
  • Dry fruits - 2 tbsp (cashew, almond, pistachio, chopped)
  • Cherries - for garnish

Steps:

  1. Blend mango pieces, milk, and sugar into a thick shake.
  2. Pour into a tall glass, leaving some space on top.
  3. Add a scoop of vanilla ice cream over the shake.
  4. Garnish with dry fruits and cherries.
  5. Serve chilled with a straw and spoon.

Strawberry Shake

A refreshing and creamy milkshake made with fresh strawberries.

Ingredients:

  • Strawberries - 1 cup (fresh or frozen)
  • Milk - 1 cup (chilled)
  • Sugar - 2 tbsp
  • Vanilla ice cream - 1 scoop (optional)
  • Ice cubes - 2 to 3

Steps:

  1. Wash and chop the strawberries.
  2. Blend strawberries, milk, sugar, and ice cubes until smooth.
  3. Add ice cream if desired and blend again.
  4. Pour into a glass and garnish with a strawberry slice.
  5. Serve chilled.

Chai

A hot and aromatic Indian tea made with milk, tea leaves, and spices.

Ingredients:

  • Water - 1 cup
  • Milk - 1 cup
  • Tea leaves - 2 tsp
  • Sugar - 2 tsp (or to taste)
  • Ginger - 1 small piece (crushed)
  • Cardamom - 2 pods (crushed)

Steps:

  1. Boil water with ginger and cardamom.
  2. Add tea leaves and simmer for 2 minutes.
  3. Pour in milk and bring to a boil.
  4. Add sugar and stir well.
  5. Strain into cups and serve hot.
