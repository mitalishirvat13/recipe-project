Sprouts Salad

A protein-rich, refreshing salad with crunchy veggies.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed sprouts (moong, chana, etc.)
  • 1 onion, 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 cucumber (chopped)
  • 1 green chili, coriander leaves
  • Lemon juice, salt, chaat masala

Steps:

  1. Boil sprouts slightly or keep raw for crunch.
  2. Mix with chopped onion, tomato, cucumber.
  3. Add lemon juice, salt & chaat masala.
  4. Garnish with coriander & serve fresh.

Oats Upma

A light and wholesome breakfast made with oats & vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 onion, 1 carrot, beans, peas (chopped)
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds, curry leaves
  • 1 green chili, ginger (grated)
  • 1 cup water, salt to taste

Avocado Toast

A quick, healthy & delicious toast topped with creamy avocado spread.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices whole-grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
  • Optional: chili flakes, cherry tomatoes, olive oil

Steps:

  1. Toast bread slices until crispy.
  2. Mash avocado in a bowl with lemon juice, salt & pepper.
  3. Spread the mixture evenly on toasted bread.
  4. Top with cherry tomatoes, chili flakes or olive oil (optional).
  5. Serve immediately as a healthy snack or breakfast.
