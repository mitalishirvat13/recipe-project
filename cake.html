Rasmalai Cake

Soft sponge cake layered with whipped cream, rasmalai pieces, and dry fruits.

Ingredients:

  • Cake base - 1 (vanilla sponge)
  • Rasmalai - 5-6 pieces
  • Whipped cream - 2 cups
  • Dry fruits - chopped
  • Saffron strands (optional)

Steps:

  1. Slice cake into 2 layers.
  2. Soak with rasmalai milk syrup.
  3. Layer with cream & rasmalai pieces.
  4. Frost with cream, garnish with dry fruits & saffron.
  5. Chill & serve cold.

Chocolate Cake

Soft, moist chocolate cake layered with rich chocolate frosting.

Ingredients:

  • Flour - 1.5 cups
  • Cocoa powder - 1/2 cup
  • Sugar - 1 cup
  • Milk - 1 cup
  • Butter - 1/2 cup (melted)
  • Baking powder - 1.5 tsp
  • Chocolate frosting - 1 cup

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, cocoa, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. Add milk and butter, whisk to smooth batter.
  3. Bake at 180°C for 30-35 mins.
  4. Cool, then layer and cover with chocolate frosting.
  5. Decorate & serve.

Vanilla Cake

Soft, fluffy vanilla cake perfect for tea-time or celebrations.

Ingredients:

  • Flour - 1.5 cups
  • Sugar - 1 cup
  • Milk - 1 cup
  • Butter - 1/2 cup (melted)
  • Baking powder - 1.5 tsp
  • Vanilla essence - 1 tsp

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. Add milk, butter, and vanilla essence; whisk smooth.
  3. Bake at 180°C for 30-35 mins.
  4. Cool, slice & serve.
