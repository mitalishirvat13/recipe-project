Chinese

Noodles

Quick and tasty stir-fried noodles with vegetables, perfect for a snack or meal.

Ingredients:

  • Instant noodles or egg noodles - 1 pack
  • Carrot - 1 small, julienned
  • Capsicum - 1 small, sliced
  • Cabbage - 1/2 cup, shredded
  • Onion - 1 small, sliced
  • Garlic - 2 cloves, minced
  • Soy sauce - 1 tbsp
  • Chili sauce (optional) - 1 tsp
  • Oil - 1 tbsp
  • Salt - to taste

Steps:

  1. Boil the noodles as per the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add garlic and onion, sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add carrots, capsicum, and cabbage. Stir-fry for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Add the cooked noodles, soy sauce, chili sauce, and salt. Toss everything together for 2 minutes.
  5. Serve hot as a snack or main dish.

Fried Rice

Flavorful stir-fried rice with vegetables and light seasoning, perfect as a quick meal.

Ingredients:

  • Cooked rice - 2 cups (preferably cooled)
  • Carrot - 1 small, diced
  • Capsicum - 1 small, diced
  • Green peas - 1/4 cup
  • Onion - 1 small, chopped
  • Garlic - 2 cloves, minced
  • Soy sauce - 1 tbsp
  • Oil - 1–2 tbsp
  • Salt and pepper - to taste

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add carrot, capsicum, and peas. Cook for 2–3 minutes.
  3. Add the cooked rice, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Stir-fry for 3–4 minutes until well mixed.
  4. Serve hot as a main dish or side dish.

Manchurian

Fried veggie balls in tangy spicy sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Cabbage - 1/2 cup
  • Carrot - 1/2 cup
  • Capsicum - 1/4 cup
  • Flour - 3 tbsp
  • Cornflour - 2 tbsp
  • Oil - for frying
  • Onion - 1 small
  • Garlic - 2 cloves
  • Soy sauce - 1 tbsp

Steps:

  1. Mix veggies, flour, cornflour, salt; form balls.
  2. Deep fry balls until golden; drain.
  3. Sauté onion & garlic, add sauces & water, simmer.
  4. Add fried balls, coat with sauce, serve hot.
