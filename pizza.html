Corn Pizza

Cheesy pizza topped with sweet corn and herbs.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza base
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Boiled sweet corn
  • Oregano & chili flakes
  • Olive oil

Steps:

  1. Spread pizza sauce on the base.
  2. Add mozzarella cheese and corn.
  3. Drizzle olive oil on top.
  4. Bake at 200°C for 10–12 mins.
  5. Sprinkle oregano & chili flakes before serving.

Paneer Pizza

A classic Italian pizza with fresh tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and basil leaves.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza base - 1 (store-bought or homemade)
  • Tomato sauce - 3 tbsp
  • Mozzarella cheese - 1/2 cup (grated)
  • Fresh basil leaves - 4–5
  • Olive oil - 1 tsp
  • Salt - a pinch
  • Oregano & chili flakes - for garnish

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (392°F).
  2. Spread tomato sauce evenly on the pizza base.
  3. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese generously on top.
  4. Add fresh basil leaves and drizzle a little olive oil.
  5. Bake for 10–12 minutes until the cheese melts and crust is golden.
  6. Sprinkle oregano & chili flakes, then serve hot.
