Sweets

Jalebi

Ingredients:

  1. Maida
  2. Sugar
  3. Water
  4. Ghee
  5. Yogurt

Recipe Steps:

  1. Mix maida and yogurt, ferment 4-5 hours.
  2. Prepare the batter, heat ghee.
  3. Make spirals and deep fry.
  4. Soak fried jalebis in sugar syrup for 5 mins.

Calories: 150 kcal per piece

Laddu

Ingredients:

  1. Gram flour
  2. Ghee
  3. Sugar
  4. Cardamom

Recipe Steps:

  1. Roast gram flour in ghee until golden.
  2. Add sugar and cardamom powder.
  3. Shape into round balls.

Calories: 120 kcal per piece

Gulab Jamun

Ingredients:

  1. Khoya
  2. Maida
  3. Milk
  4. Sugar
  5. Water
  6. Cardamom

Recipe Steps:

  1. Make a dough of khoya and maida.
  2. Shape into balls, deep fry.
  3. Prepare sugar syrup.
  4. Soak fried balls in syrup for 10 mins.

Calories: 200 kcal per piece
