About Us

HI, We are Anshu & Mitali

And World of Recipes is our little corner of the internet!

We're the voice, author, and creator behind World of Recipes. What started as a simple kitchen experiment has blossomed into World of Recipes — where flavor meets simplicity. Born from two friends’ love for cooking and teaching, we craft foolproof recipes, smart tips, and playful food stories that spark joy in every kitchen. Our recipes are made for real life — busy mornings, tiny kitchens, and big appetites — so you can whip up dishes that are delicious, doable, and downright delightful.
