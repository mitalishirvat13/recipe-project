Tomato Soup

A classic, tangy and creamy soup made with fresh tomatoes, perfect as a starter.

Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes - 4 large (chopped)
  • Onion - 1 small (chopped)
  • Garlic - 3 cloves
  • Butter - 1 tbsp
  • Black pepper - 1/2 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Sugar - 1/2 tsp (optional)
  • Cream - 2 tbsp (for garnish, optional)
  • Water/vegetable stock - 2 cups

Steps:

  1. Heat butter in a pan, sauté onion and garlic until soft.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and cook for 5–7 minutes.
  3. Pour water/stock, add salt and pepper, simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Blend the mixture into a smooth puree and strain if needed.
  5. Reheat, adjust seasoning, and serve hot with cream on top.

Broccoli Soup

A healthy, creamy, and comforting soup made with fresh broccoli, perfect for a light meal or starter.

Ingredients:

  • Broccoli - 2 cups (chopped)
  • Onion - 1 medium (chopped)
  • Garlic - 3 cloves
  • Butter/Olive oil - 1 tbsp
  • Milk - 1 cup (or cream for extra richness)
  • Vegetable stock/water - 2 cups
  • Black pepper - 1/2 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Cheese - 2 tbsp (optional, for garnish)

Steps:

  1. Heat butter/olive oil in a pan, sauté onion and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add chopped broccoli and cook for 4–5 minutes.
  3. Pour in vegetable stock/water, season with salt and pepper, and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Blend the mixture into a smooth puree.
  5. Stir in milk/cream, reheat, and adjust seasoning.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with cheese or croutons.

Mushroom Soup

A rich, earthy, and creamy soup made with fresh mushrooms, perfect for cozy evenings.

Ingredients:

  • Mushrooms - 200g (sliced)
  • Onion - 1 small (chopped)
  • Garlic - 3 cloves
  • Butter - 1 tbsp
  • Vegetable stock/water - 2 cups
  • Milk/cream - 1 cup
  • Black pepper - 1/2 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Fresh parsley - 1 tbsp (for garnish, optional)

Steps:

  1. Heat butter in a pan, sauté onion and garlic until golden.
  2. Add sliced mushrooms and cook until soft and slightly browned.
  3. Pour in vegetable stock/water, season with salt and pepper, and simmer for 8–10 minutes.
  4. Blend the mixture into a smooth puree (leave some mushroom pieces if you like texture).
  5. Stir in milk/cream, reheat, and adjust seasoning.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley or a swirl of cream.
